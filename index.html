FAQ

How long does installation take?

Small residential systems can be installed in ~2–4 days after approvals and material readiness. Commercial timelines vary with size and site conditions.

Do you help with net‑metering?

Yes. We provide documentation support and guidance as per your state DISCOM’s process.

What maintenance is required?

Regular cleaning and periodic electrical checks are recommended. Our AMC plans keep your system healthy and productive.
